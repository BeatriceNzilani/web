Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers:

  • How do I place an order?
    To place an order, simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept the following payment methods:
    • Mobile Money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN Mobile Money)
    • Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard
    • Debit cards
    • Bank transfer
    • Cash on Delivery (available in select locations)
  • Can I change or cancel my order?
    To change or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible with your order details. We'll do our best to accommodate your request before the order is processed and shipped.
  • your products covered by a warranty? Yes, many of our products are covered by a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty details can usually be found on the product page or included in the product packaging. Please contact us if you need further assistance with warranty claims.
  • What should I do if I receive a damaged or defective item? If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team immediately with photos of the item and its packaging. We'll work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible, whether it's through a replacement, refund, or exchange.
